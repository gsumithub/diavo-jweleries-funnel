Our Story & Philosophy

Founded by Anushka Agarwal & Rishabh Agarwal, Diavo Jewels represents a pursuit of handcrafted luxury. What started as a bespoke atelier designing private collections in New Delhi DLF Midtown has flourished into a trusted brand. Every ornament we design is treated as a wearable work of art, merging heritage Indian jewelry techniques with clean, contemporary design profiles.

We work exclusively with certified conflict-free natural diamonds, hallmarked 18K yellow gold, and high-purity sterling silver to make sure your investments endure for generations.
